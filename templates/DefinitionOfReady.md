# Definition of Ready

## 1. Scope is explicit

- Problem statement is clear.
- In-scope and out-of-scope boundaries are documented.

## 2. Business or operational value is stated

- Expected outcome is measurable.

## 3. Acceptance criteria

- Acceptance criteria in the task are objective and testable.
- Edge cases and failure behavior are listed where relevant.

## 4. Dependencies are identified

- Upstream and downstream tasks, systems, and owners are known.
- Blocking dependencies are either resolved or explicitly tracked.

## 5. Owner and reviewers are assigned

- One direct owner.
- At least one reviewer role identified.

## 6. Effort estimate exists

- Relative estimate or time estimate is documented.

## 7. Risk and rollback intent are captured

- Rollback or mitigation approach is defined for risky changes (for example database migrations).
